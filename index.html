// 01 about

Operations is where I'm most at home.

For 3+ years at a Yazaki Japan affiliate I've owned the production server estate behind daily operations for ~700 staff — and as a one-team IT shop, that means the whole stack: bare-metal and Linux administration up through the applications running on it. I provision and harden virtualized infrastructure, replace manual builds with declarative automation, and ship the internal web tools and scripts that keep it reliable and observable.

Operator first, with a working developer's hands — I write the Laravel/Vue tooling and Bash/Python automation myself rather than waiting on someone else to. I prefer systems that explain themselves: parameterized templates, reproducible provisioning, and dashboards over guesswork. Currently extending that into cloud, working toward AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner with Terraform Associate and CKA next.
